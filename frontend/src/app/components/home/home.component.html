Hi there! Welcome to Codecraft. Codecraft is an educational website intended to help students improve and develop their coding skills and learn various topics related to computer science. We felt, the old codecraft was too limited in content and features. Also scalability was an issue due to technology barriers. Solving the above problems we launch this site with robust technology, and new features to improve your learning experience.

What's New

The learn section provides you with comprehensive content on various programming launguages, data structures, algorithms and other topics related to computer science.

The discuss section now gives you the power to interact with others and clear all the confusion and questions which you may have. It works something like the discuss platform on codechef and other coding websites.

The site is not limited to only learning and discussion. We also have something for the ones who love to share their knowlege. The blog section allowes you to write on any topic that you would like to share with others.


Codecraft is armed with its own judge and code editor. You can run, share and search code. So go ahead, code your heart out and also share your submissions to help other students learn from you.

The notification engine will give you regular updates on new content being added to the site. You would also be notified about any comments on your blogs and codes, or answers on your questions.

Now you can now connect with other users of the website. The find people section allowes you to search poeple by their names and their skills. You can visit profiles of users working with technologies you wish to learn.

Technology Under The Hood

The site is built completly on the MEAN stack applying the MVC architecture. The acronym stands for MongoDB, Express, Angular and NodeJS. The complete stack is based on only one launguage, Javascript. MongoDB, a NoSql database has been used to store the entire content of the site. Angular is a front-end javascript based framework used to create single page dynamic websites. NodeJs, a javascript runtime has been used for writing the backend logic and routes. Express is the node web-applicaion framework used to configure the server.

The Editor

The site uses TinyMCE editor for all its editing tasks. It is an open source WYSIWYG editor which works great on almost all platforms. Do format your text while writing blogs or questions with the various options available on the editor. To add images, simply drag and drop images into the editor. Note:- Users will require internet connection to view the images, as image upload is not supported at the moment.
